About MizoFlo

Reliable Piping. Reliable Partners.

MizoFlo Pipes & Fittings is a construction materials brand operating under AKap Group, focused on manufacturing and distributing high-quality piping systems and fittings for residential, commercial, and industrial use.

MizoFlo is built on the same principles that guide every AKap Group company — quality without compromise, and a partner-first approach to distribution. Every partner in the MizoFlo network is tracked through a structured coding system, ensuring transparency and accountability from factory to fitting.
